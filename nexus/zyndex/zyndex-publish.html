Host/publish your Zyndex

You can host your Zyndex however you want. If you have a server or web space, you can simply upload your Zyndex there and then access it with the address of your web space. You can also move it into subfolders if you want. But remember that whenever you want to use Zyndex “officially”, you have to access it with the full URL, including the “/index.json” at the end.
